Project Overview

About Agent for History Matching

Agent for History Matching is a deep learning-based reservoir history matching system developed during my internship at Saudi Aramco. The project focuses on using advanced machine learning techniques to improve the accuracy and efficiency of reservoir modeling.

The system implements agent design and development for reservoir history matching, with model optimization and fine-tuning based on expert labels. This approach significantly reduces the time and computational resources required for traditional history matching methods.

By leveraging deep learning algorithms, the system can better capture complex reservoir behaviors and provide more accurate predictions, ultimately leading to better decision-making in oil and gas operations.
