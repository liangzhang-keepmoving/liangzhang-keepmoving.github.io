My Research Journey in AI and Networking

Welcome to my first blog post! I'm excited to share my research experiences and insights in the fields of artificial intelligence and networking.

The Intersection of AI and Networking

Over the past few years, I've been exploring the fascinating intersection of AI and networking technologies. This interdisciplinary area has tremendous potential for solving complex problems in communication systems, from optimizing network performance to enabling intelligent edge devices.

Current Focus Areas

My current work primarily focuses on several key areas:

1. **Federated Learning** - Developing privacy-preserving machine learning approaches for edge computing environments

2. **LLM Optimization** - Creating techniques to accelerate inference and improve efficiency of large language models

3. **Distributed AI Systems** - Building scalable frameworks for training and deploying AI models across geographically distributed infrastructure
